Refunds Policy

Last Updated: 09/05/2024 At Physikul Health.

Thank you for choosing Physikul Health for your online Pilates classes. We are committed to providing you with exceptional service and support throughout your fitness journey. To ensure clarity and transparency, we have established the following refunds policy:

‍Cancellation Policy:
‍
- Clients must cancel their booking at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled class time to qualify for a refund.Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled class time will not be eligible for a refund.

‍Refund Process:
‍
- Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during booking.To request a refund, clients should contact us via email at physikul.health@gmail.com.
- Refund requests must include the client's name, booking details, and reason for cancellation.
- Refunds will be processed within 5 business days from the date of the cancellation request.

‍Exceptions:
‍
- Refunds may be considered at the discretion of Physikul Health in cases of technical issues or unforeseen circumstances preventing attendance.
- If a class is canceled by Physikul Health due to instructor unavailability or technical difficulties, clients will receive a full refund.

‍No-show Policy:
‍
- Clients who fail to attend a scheduled class without prior 24hr notice will not be eligible for a refund.

‍Policy Changes:
‍
- Physikul Health reserves the right to amend or update this refunds policy at any time without prior notice.
- Any changes will take effect immediately upon posting on our website.
- By booking a class with Physikul Health, you acknowledge and agree to comply with the terms and conditions outlined in this refunds policy.
- If you have any questions or require further assistance, please feel free to reach out to us.

Thank you for choosing Physikul Health for your fitness needs.
